VNE Verion 0.3

Key Commands:

1	Control Object 1
2	Control Object 2
3	Control Object 3

w	Speed up
s	Slow Down

e	Speed Up Spin
d	Slow Down Spin

x	Spin about x-axis
y	Spin about y-axis
z	Spin about z-axis

]	Enable Vortex (spiral to origin)
[	Disable Vortex

i	accelerate up
k	accelerate down
j	accelerate left
l	accelerate right

t	move up
g	move down
f	move left
h	move right

v	zoom camera in	(z-axis)
c	zoom camera out (z-axis)

b,n, ,, . : control x&y position of camera (this control is funky now)

R	reset camera position (z = 5, pointed down at origin)
